The Property

NOT A MALL.

American Dream is the second-largest commercial complex in the United States — and the only one where entertainment outweighs retail. This structural distinction makes it resistant to e-commerce attrition. Consumers cannot download a roller coaster or stream an indoor ski slope.


The New York metro catchment of 20.1 million people includes the highest concentration of ultra-high-net-worth households in the country. New Jersey levies zero sales tax on clothing and footwear — a decisive advantage pulling luxury tourists across the Hudson for high-margin purchases.
